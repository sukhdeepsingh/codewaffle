About me

Work!

I am Sukhi, currently residing in Dresden, and working at BioInnovations - Zentrum der Technischen Universität Dresden. My research interests are computational epigenetics, investigating histone modifications and transcriptional machinery in mouse ESCs and NSCs.







Currently, I have been developing computational pipelines to handle and analyse the plethora of NGS datasets(ChIP-Seq, RNA-Seq) utilizing High Performance computing.
